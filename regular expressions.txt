Regular expressions:

a regular expression is a sequence of characters that match a pattern. 

[]       represent a character
^        matches the begining
$        matches the ending
*        number of occurrences(zero also )
+        number of occurances(from 1)
.        to match a character




[0-9]-> any digit
[a-z]-> any lower case alphabet
[2468]->all single digit multiples of 2
^[0-9]{1}$-> only single digit numbers
^[0-9]{3}$-> only 3 digit numbers
[0-9]*0$-> all multiples of 10
^([1-9][0-9]*[05] [5] -> all multiples of 5
(word)-> seaching for a word
